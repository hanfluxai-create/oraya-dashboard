## Why we're calling you, in one sentence
We've built a voice-AI qualifier that takes a cold or warm lead, calls them inside 60 seconds, qualifies intent (auto / home / life / commercial / health), and only transfers a buying-ready prospect to a licensed agent — and we want you to either buy the leads or put us on a weekly retainer, whichever is better economics for your team.

## The three numbers that matter for you
- **Speed-to-contact:** 47 seconds median from form-fill to qualifier dialing. Industry is 8+ hours.
- **Qualification conversion:** voice-qualified leads close at 2.4–3.1× vs raw form leads (internal Apr test cohort, n=86).
- **Cost:** $25–$50 per qualified lead on pay-per, or $1,000/week retainer with unlimited volume + priority routing.

## What it replaces
- The SDR seat you're about to hire (or already hate paying).
- The 11 PM "are you still interested?" calls that your appointment setter won't make.
- The lead-vendor bill where 7 of 10 leads are dead on arrival.

## What it does not replace
- You closing. The qualifier hands off a warm transfer, live, to a licensed agent on your team. The close stays human.
- Your compliance stack. We stay on the qualifying side of the TCPA line — no quoting, no binding, no rate disclosure.

## How the first two weeks would look
1. **Week 1 — one-way handshake.** We take 25 leads from your existing source (whatever's already coming in). We voice-qualify. You take the warm transfers. We measure: % answered, % qualified, % warm-transferred, % closed.
2. **Week 2 — two-way handshake.** If week 1 hits bar, we start feeding our own sourced leads into your team under either model (per-lead or retainer).
3. **Week 3+ — only if the numbers are there.** Scale to 100 leads/week. If not, we unwind with no hard feelings and you keep the infra notes.

## What we need from you on the call
- Your current lead cost and close rate (rough — we don't need a spreadsheet).
- Your agent count and how warm transfers route today.
- The one verticals mix you want first (home+auto bundle? commercial? Medicare?).
- Whichever option lines up with your incentive math: per-lead or retainer.

## What you get from us on the call
- 5–7 minute live demo: we call your phone, run a mock lead through the qualifier, you listen end-to-end.
- A one-page pricing sheet with the per-lead vs retainer break-even math for your volume.
- A trial window: first 25 qualified leads free if you commit to a feedback call at end of week 1.

## The ask, in one line
**"Pick Saturday or Sunday, 20 minutes. I'll call you."**
