Diagnostic SQL (R13 verdict): Q1 total count, Q2 S-Class variant count (where trim ~ '^S[0-9]{3}'), Q3 cron freshness (max(updated_at)), Q4 2026 model-year coverage. Q2=0 means sync-miss. Q2>0 and Q4<dealer-site-count means query-tool fault. Q3 age > 24h means cron failing.
