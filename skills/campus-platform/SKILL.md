# Skill: Campus Experience Platform
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Vision
Event management SaaS → full campus lifecycle platform (recruitment to alumni)

## Pricing Tiers
Fest Starter Kit: $299-$499 (500 attendees max, basic events + QR check-in)
Academic Suite: $999-$1,999 (2,000 attendees, speakers, polling, lead retrieval)
Total Campus: $4,999-$9,999/yr (unlimited events, alumni/career, white-label)

## À La Carte Modules
Event Engine $199 | Registration $99 | Ticketing $149 | Polling $199 | Speaker Mgmt $149
Schedule Builder $199 | Analytics $249 | Mobile App $299 | Credentialing $199
Alumni Integration $399 | Custom Branding $199

## Tech Stack
Backend: Node.js/Express (microservices) | Frontend: React/Next.js
Real-time: Socket.io | Queue: Bull Queue | Monitoring: Prometheus/Grafana
DB: PostgreSQL + ClickHouse + Redis + S3

## Roadmap
Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Core — events, registration, QR, basic analytics
Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Advanced — speakers, polling, mobile, analytics
Phase 3+: Alumni, career services, white-label, enterprise
